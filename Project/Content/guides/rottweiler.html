Rottweilers are strong, intelligent, and confident dogs. They are loyal protectors and make excellent family companions when trained and socialized properly. Rottweilers need consistent guidance and exercise to stay happy and healthy.

ü•ó Feeding

  • Feed high-quality, protein-rich dog food formulated for large breeds.
  • Divide meals into two portions daily to prevent bloating and overeating.
  • Provide fresh water at all times and monitor their weight.

üè° Living Environment

  • Rottweilers require a spacious environment with room to move and exercise.
  • They enjoy outdoor playtime but also need indoor companionship.
  • Provide secure fencing if they spend time outside to ensure safety.

üßº Health & Grooming

  • Brush their short coat weekly to remove loose hair and maintain skin health.
  • Check ears, teeth, and nails regularly to prevent infections or issues.
  • Schedule regular vet visits and vaccinations, and monitor for hip and joint issues.

üíñ Love & Attention

  • Rottweilers are loyal and affectionate with family members.
  • They require early socialization and training to be gentle and well-behaved.
  • Spend time on training, bonding, and play to keep them mentally and physically stimulated.
